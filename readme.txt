== Description ==

YouSayToo auto-publishing plugin and backlink all in one. It will automatically publish your posts in your YouSayToo account with credits to your blog. No changing of the blog feed settings required.

Your blog needs to be added to YouSayToo in order for the plugin to work. [Register and add your blog now!](http://www.yousaytoo.com)

Email us at feedback@yousaytoo.com if you have any questions.

== Installation ==

Your blog needs to be added to YouSayToo in order for the plugin to work. [Register and add your blog now!](http://www.yousaytoo.com)

1. Upload yousaytoo.php to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Enter API Key on Plugin Settings page. The API key will be provided when you add your blog to YouSayToo.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What do I need to do to make this plugin work? =

You register and add your blog to YouSayToo
Install the plugin

= Where do I get the API key? =

The API key will be provided when you add your blog to YouSayToo.

= Where will my post be imported? = 

Posts from your blog will be imported into your YouSayToo account.

= Why is it a good idea to import my posts? = 

You'll expand your readership, get more fans and earn AdSense and Infolinks revenue from your posts. [Read more](http://www.yousaytoo.com/about/add_your_blog)

= Do I need to put a backlink separately with the YouSayToo auto-publishing plugin? =

No, the YouSayToo plugin serves as a backlink.

= When will my posts be imported into my YouSayToo account? =

Your posts will be imported within 10 minutes after you publish them on your blog.

= Will my old posts be imported? = 

No, only your new posts will be imported.

= Do I need to change my blog feed settings? = 

No, you can keep your blog feed settings as they are.

= Is there a step by step instruction I can view to help me install the plugin? =

2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
